Obsessed with

  • How to design refusal states in conversational AI products — currently the most underdesigned part of the field. Where AI confidently says "I don't know" is more important than where it confidently answers.
  • The "system as contract" framing — design systems are technical contracts, not preferences. Consequence: deviation is a bug, not an opinion.
  • The boundary between AI-generated and human-committed in regulated workflows. AI surfaces signals; humans commit decisions. The interface is where this contract lives.
  • Whether senior PDs in 2026 should code or just be code-fluent. Currently leaning toward "ship in code if you can; understand it deeply if you can't."
